A Lifelong Quest for Solitary Giants

It all started in the fog-drenched coastal forests of Northern California when I first stood beneath Hyperion and General Sherman. Standing in the shadow of living giants that took root during the Bronze Age fundamentally reshaped my appreciation for time, ecology, and nature's enduring quiet strength.

Since that morning, I became driven to map and preserve the memory of Earth’s most exceptional trees. From 4,800-year-old Bristlecone Pines clinging to wind-sculpted white mountains, to sacred Sri Lankan Bodhi trees planted in 288 BC, every solitary tree tells a chapter of human and geological history.

"Trees are sanctuaries. Whoever knows how to speak to them, whoever knows how to listen to them, can learn the ultimate truth of endurance."

Treeary was born as an open botanical field journal and global atlas. Every record in our archive carries verified geographic coordinates, estimated population status records, historical anecdotes, and botanical notes.

Creator's Personal Favorites

The three most legendary, personal, and unforgettable solitary trees in my journal.

🪦 R.I.P.

The Golden Spruce

Kiidk'yaas — Haida Gwaii, British Columbia

A legendary 300-year-old Sitka spruce with unique golden needles caused by a rare genetic mutation. Sacred to the Haida people, it stood as a natural wonder until its tragic felling in 1997. R.I.P. to a true forest wonder.

Status: 14,892 R.I.P.s
🪦 R.I.P.

The Tree of Ténéré

Sahara Desert, Niger

Once known as the most isolated tree on Earth—standing completely alone with no other tree within 250 miles (400 km). Its roots tapped into a water table 115 ft underground. Knocked down by a truck in 1973. R.I.P.

Status: 21,405 R.I.P.s
🌲 DISCOVERED BY ME!

World's Largest White Spruce

Picea glauca — Secret Northern Wilderness

My proudest field discovery! While expeditioning deep into unmapped northern boreal river valleys, I stumbled upon this titan White Spruce measuring an unheard-of 168 ft tall and 14.2 ft in circumference!

Record Status: 🟢 Verified Champion
